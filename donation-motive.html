Why the Donation Fund?

Welcome to the Donation Fund initiative! This isn't just a number ticking up every minute. It's a commitment, a reminder, and a meaningful gesture towards a brighter future.

Our Purpose

The idea is simple yet impactful: for every minute that passes, ₹1 is added to the fund. This fund symbolizes the value of time and how even small contributions can accumulate into something significant over the years.

Where Will the Money Go?

When the fund reaches its milestones, the collected amount will be directed towards causes that matter — whether it's supporting education, aiding healthcare, or empowering local communities. Every rupee counts, and every contribution has the potential to change lives.

How Can You Get Involved?

Stay tuned for updates and opportunities to contribute or suggest causes that resonate with you. Together, we can make a difference, one minute at a time.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Your support and encouragement mean the world and are crucial in turning this vision into reality!
